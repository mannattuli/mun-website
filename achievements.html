OUR ACHIEVEMENTS


The MUN society is always active in competitions and we have won accolades in various
competitions since the very beginning. Inspite of the pandemic we are still active in online
interschool and international MUNs and winning laurels.

We are also organising online workshops and online intra MUNs for training new MUNers.
